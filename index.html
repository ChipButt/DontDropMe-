    How to play

    Rules

    • Tap directly on a ball to push it away from your tap point.
    • Every 3 upward hits, all active balls shrink and gravity rises.
    • Guide balls into gems and powerups. Tapping items does nothing.
    • Levels end only when every active ball has hit the floor.
    • Stars are awarded after failure based on your final score.
